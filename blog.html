December 25, 2014

Christmas Day Press Release!

PRESS RELEASE - 25 DECEMBER 2014 - KIDSCONNECTNET.COM


CEO Tyler Malley announced that this Christmas, KidsConnect Staff Members and developers will not be working through the new year to allow for family time around the Holidays, but even with our staff off, we feel that today is a good time to announce our progress and any updates we have been waiting to announce.


Our Support Center

We shut down our support center at http://support.kidsconnectnet.com on Wednesday, December 17, at 2:00pm. We decided that due to our low staff count that all support tickets will be posted directly to admins on the site using a contact form. If you have any problems, please email Tyler Malley, CEO until we launch our new contact page.


Our Hosting

KidsConnectNET.com has moved its hosting over to a new home - DreamHost. We feel that this change will allow for bigger boundaries and that new horizons to be reached. At DreamHost, we now occupy our own Virtual Server which allows us to install chat clients and make back-end changes that are needed prior to our launch! We will continue to update you on this exciting change!


KidsConnect's "Vision"

We have been working on tweaking what we want on KidsConnect/how we want certain things to be made on KidsConnect! We cannot get into details yet, but you may have noticed minor changes implied or indicated. We will keep you posted on this matter!
